Welcome!

For nearly eight years, Green Property Management (GPM) has been at the forefront of providing affordable housing for families in the city of Atlanta. Over the past 20 years, Atlanta population has tripled making affordable housing difficult to find. To combat this shortage, we are introducing the Green Re-purposing Initiative. The City of Atlanta has given GPM authority to convert largely unused park property into affordable housing.

we aren't building houses. we're building homes.

Over the next 5 years, we will be converting over 5000 acres of public space into much needed housing to support Atlanta's growing population.
